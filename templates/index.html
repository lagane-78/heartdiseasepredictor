Heart disease is the leading cause of death worldwide. Definitive diagnosis of heart disease is challenging due to its many and complexly interrelated manifestation of symptoms, manifestations, and risk factors.

In this demonstration project, we used machine learning algorithms to develop a heart disease prediction tool to allow users to predict the presence of heart disease based on their individual risk factors, symptoms, test, and examination results.
